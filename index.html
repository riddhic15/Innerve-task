About Us

Innerve, the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW), started in 2015 and all these years it has been witnessing phenomenal gathering, is back with it's SIXTH EDITION. Innerve has an overall reach of 100 thousand plus students in Delhi and NCR. Leaded by a student force of 200 passionate girls. Spread over a span of two days, it aims to become a platform for students to test their engineering skills in different fields and unleash their horses. The sole vision of Innerve is to boll down theories into applications and shape a platform where students could interact and lay down stronger ties.

Every year we come up BIGGER, BETTER and STRONGER.
